Privacy Policy — FinSage

FinSphere Solutions (“FinSphere Solutions”, “FinSage”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal and financial information processed through the FinSage platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, secured, and disclosed when users access or use the FinSage platform and related services.

Information is processed to:

  • Maintain platform functionality

  • Comply with legal obligations

Customers retain ownership of their financial and business data.

FinSphere Solutions processes data solely for the purpose of providing FinSage services and related operational support.

5. Security Measures

FinSage implements reasonable technical and organisational security measures, including:

FinSphere Solutions treats customer information as confidential and limits access to authorised personnel only where operationally necessary.

FinSage aims to align with the principles of the Protection of Personal Information Act (“POPIA”) of South Africa.

Users may request:

FinSphere Solutions does not sell customer data.
